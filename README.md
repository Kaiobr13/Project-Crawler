## Introduction
**Project Name: Project McQueen**

The purpose of this project is to create a vehicle and surpass the expectation of our teachers. We aim to create a machine which is able to cargo transport through a specified route in two ways: manual driving and auto driving, we wish to add a few sensors to assist each moment.

## Objectives

**What is the purpose?**

We are being evaluated in a range of aspects based on the tech industry as such as teamwork, know-how of a computer engineering project, agile methodology etc. We are going to try to excel in each area to prove our potential to ourselves. We are going to contribute with the 2nd year of Design course as they are going to create the cargo holder for our vehicle.


**Our objectives are:**

- Understanding of what the market needs therefore we can apply to our project
- Features to see speed, distance traveled, loaded weight and a few more options which we are still contemplating
- We plan to add a GPS system and other sensors to prevent detect a potential fall and its damage.

## Where which subject will be included

**Computação Física e IOT**

This subject is going to be have the main role this semester because we are going to learn how to program the vehicle to surpass the variety of tasks proposed by our teachers. All phases of building and welding parts is the focus here.

**Project Factory**

Here we are going to face real industry problems. The brainstorming, contribute with various teams to reach the goal (vehicle with all features), time scheduling etc. It is going to polish our skills to deal with the market in a near future.

**Sistemas Operativos**

The plan to add a GPS system is to satisfy our needs within this class, we need to have it working at the end so the auto driving feature can work well.
